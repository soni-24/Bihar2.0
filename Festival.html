Celebrate the festival of purity, devotion, and gratitude dedicated to the Sun God, only in the heart of Bihar.

Chhath Puja

Chhath is a major festival in Bihar, dedicated to the Sun God. It is celebrated with fasting, holy bathing, and offering prayers at riverbanks.

Holi

Holi, the festival of colors, is celebrated with joy and brotherhood. People throw colors, sing songs, and share sweets.

Diwali

Diwali is the festival of lights. Homes are decorated with diyas, rangoli, and fireworks light up the night sky.

Jitiya

Jitiya is a festival observed by mothers for the well-being and long life of their children. It includes fasting and rituals.

Sama-Chakeva

This festival is celebrated by girls in Mithila. It symbolizes the bond between brothers and sisters with folk songs and clay idols.
